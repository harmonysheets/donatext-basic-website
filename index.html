Terms & Conditions

By texting DONATEXT, you agree to receive transactional responses from our phone and email systems regarding your donations. To stop receiving messages, reply 'STOP' at any time.

We will not send promotional messages or emails, only transactional updates and responses regarding your donations.

Commands:

  • LIST: Get all campaign IDs.
  • SAVE: Save a credit card.
  • UPDATE: Update a credit card.
  • REPORTS: Request reports.
  • ADD: Add phone/email.
  • DELETE: Delete phone/email/saved credit card.
  • FILE: Check your account's phones/emails/credit cards.
  • SUPPORT: Contact support.
  • MAASER: Track your Maaser.
  • PROFILE: View and update your profile.

Reply 'HELP [COMMAND]' for help on a specific command.
